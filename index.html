Mo redox lines (dashed purple on the diagram)

These show where Mo metal would be thermodynamically stable. Both lines sit below the H₂ evolution line in alkaline solution, which is why pure Mo cannot be electrodeposited from aqueous baths — HER outcompetes the Mo reduction kinetically. Ni catalyses the pathway via the surface intermediates discussed in the literature.
